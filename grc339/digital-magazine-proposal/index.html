Introduction

A silly little garden

We aim to enlighten new plant parents on the best way to keep their darlings alive in a fun and engaging way. Our handbooks range from tips and tricks to caring for succulents, to which regions certain plants survive the best in, to what kind of edible veggies you can grow in water in the kitchen.
